About The Game

Step into the arena where basketball meets fighting! LeBron Fighter is a 2D fighting game that combines the intensity of Street Fighter with the legendary skills of LeBron James. Choose your side - Lakers purple and gold or Heat red and black - and battle it out on the basketball court in an epic showdown!

Master powerful moves inspired by LeBron's greatest moments, build your special meter, and unleash devastating ultimates to dominate your opponent. Best of 3 rounds - may the best King win! üëë

How To Play

Game Modes

  • 1 Player vs CPU: Battle against an AI opponent
  • 2 Player Mode: Face off against a friend locally

Win Condition

Best of 3 rounds - first to win 2 rounds wins the match!

Each round lasts 90 seconds. Reduce your opponent's health to zero or have more health when time runs out.

Special Meter

Build up your special meter by dealing and taking damage. Use it to unleash powerful special moves, or fill it completely to perform the devastating ultimate attack!

Tips & Strategies

  • Mix it up: Combine punches, kicks, and special moves to keep your opponent guessing
  • Block wisely: Blocking reduces damage significantly, but you can't attack while blocking
  • Manage your meter: Save up for the ultimate, or use smaller specials to maintain pressure
  • Spacing is key: Use projectiles to control distance, or get close for heavy damage
  • Jump attacks: Time your jumps to avoid projectiles and approach safely
  • Watch the timer: If you're ahead on health, play defensively as time runs out
  • Combo potential: Land multiple hits quickly to build combo counters and special meter
