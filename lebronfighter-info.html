Step into the arena where basketball meets fighting! LeBron Fighter is a 2D fighting game that combines the intensity of Street Fighter with the legendary skills of LeBron James. Choose your side - Lakers purple and gold or Heat red and black - and battle it out on the basketball court in an epic showdown!

Master powerful moves inspired by LeBron's greatest moments, build your special meter, and unleash devastating ultimates to dominate your opponent. Best of 3 rounds - may the best King win! üëë
